Which National Football Team To Support?

There are 32 teams to pick from and here is data to help you choose which team to support

The 32 NFL teams split into two categories, the NFC (National Football Conference) and the AFC (American Football Conference). Each year the teams play for the championship of the NFC and AFC and the resulting winners of those conferences go on to play the Superbowl.
